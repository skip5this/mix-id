# Third-Party Notices

## FFmpeg and FFprobe

Cuezy packages FFmpeg and FFprobe binaries for desktop audio analysis. FFmpeg
and FFprobe are part of the FFmpeg project.

- Project: https://ffmpeg.org/
- macOS binary provider: https://ffmpeg.martin-riedl.de/
- Provider build script: https://git.martin-riedl.de/ffmpeg/build-script
- Pinned artifact manifest: `resources/ffmpeg-manifest.json`
- License: GPL-2.0-or-later
- Version currently pinned for macOS: 8.1

The bundled binaries are separate third-party components. Their exact download
URLs and SHA-256 checksums are recorded in `resources/ffmpeg-manifest.json`.
